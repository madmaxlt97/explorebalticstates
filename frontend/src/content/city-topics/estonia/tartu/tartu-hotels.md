# Top Tartu Hotels: Price/Quality

_Below is a list of hotels in Tartu that offer an excellent price-to-quality ratio, based on high guest ratings and competitive average prices. (Attention: Prices are approximate average starting prices per night, converted to EUR. Actual prices can change significantly based on the season, booking date, and day of the week. Always check current prices and exact addresses on booking services.)_

## Lydia Hotel

**Area:** Ülikooli str. 14. Centre (Near Town Hall Square). **Average Price:** ~€120 - €150. **Rating:** 9.4 / Superb (4.8/5). **Key Highlight:** The highest-rated luxury hotel in the center, praised for its quality service, location, and overall experience.

## Dorpat Hotel

**Area:** Soola str. 6. Centre (Near River Emajõgi and Central Bus Station). **Average Price:** ~€65 - €80. **Rating:** 8.6 / Very Good (4.0/5). **Key Highlight:** One of the most popular hotels in Tartu, offering comfortable accommodation and good value for its central location near the river and transport hubs.

## Hotel Tartu

**Area:** Soola str. 3. Centre (Near Train and Bus Stations). **Average Price:** ~€70 - €85. **Rating:** 8.7 / Excellent (4.3/5). **Key Highlight:** Highly-rated for its service and quality, offering great value and easy access to both the city center and transport links.

## Art Hotel Pallas by Tartuhotels

**Area:** Riia str. 4. Centre (Near Town Hall Square/City Centre). **Average Price:** ~€75 - €90. **Rating:** 8.0 / Very Good (4.0/5). **Key Highlight:** A popular hotel known for its art-themed interior and convenient location right in the city center.

## Hotel Sophia by Tartuhotels

**Area:** Lääneringtee 39. Lõunakeskus (Outskirts, near Shopping Centre). **Average Price:** ~€70 - €85. **Rating:** 8.8 / Excellent (4.4/5). **Key Highlight:** Located outside the direct city center, this hotel offers excellent quality and comfort at a good price, especially for those with a car or visiting the Lõunakeskus area.

## Hugo Apartment Hotel

**Area:** Raatuse str. 23. Centre (Kesklinn). **Average Price:** ~€60 - €75. **Rating:** High (Popular choice). **Key Highlight:** An apartment-style option, popular among travelers for its convenience, self-catering facilities, and excellent value in the city center.
