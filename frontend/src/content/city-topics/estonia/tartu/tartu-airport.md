# Best way to reach Tartu from Tallinn Airport (TLL).

_The most efficient and comfortable way to travel from Tallinn Airport to Tartu is by combining a short local tram ride with a direct intercity express bus._

## Step 1: Tallinn Airport (TLL) to Tallinn Bus Station

Your first step is to get from the airport to the Tallinn Bus Station (Tallinna bussijaam), which is the departure point for all major intercity buses to Tartu.

Mode: Tram Line 4 (Vana-Lõuna direction).

Location: The tram stop is located directly in front of the terminal building.

Destination Stop: The tram travels directly to the Tallinna bussijaam stop.

Duration: Approximately _15 minutes_.

#### Cost: _€1.50 – €2.00_.

## Step 2: Tallinn Bus Station to Tartu City Center

Once at the Tallinn Bus Station, you will take the express bus to Tartu.

Mode: Intercity Express Bus (Highly Recommended).

Main Carrier: Lux Express offers the most frequent, comfortable, and reliable service.

Departure Point: Tallinn Bus Station (Tallinna bussijaam).

Arrival Point: Tartu Bus Station (Tartu bussijaam), which is located right in the center of Tartu.

Duration: Approximately _2 hours and 30 minutes_.

#### Cost: _€10 – €15_.

_Features: Modern buses with free Wi-Fi, power outlets, and often individual media screens. Booking your ticket online in advance is strongly recommended._

## Alternative: Train Option

While the express bus is superior, a train is another option, though you depart from a different station.

### Step 1. TLL Airport to Rail Station:

Take Tram Line 4 to Balti Jaam (Tallinn's main train station), which takes about _20 minutes_.

### Step 2. Train to Tartu:

Take the Elron train from Balti Jaam to Tartu.

Total Duration: The train journey itself takes about _2 hours 30 minutes to 3 hours_.

#### Cost: _€12 – €18_.

#### Summary Recommendation: The simplest journey is Tram 4 (TLL -> Bus Station) followed by the Lux Express Bus (Tallinn -> Tartu).
