# Best Estonian Restaurants in Tartu

## Hõlm

Modern Cuisine, focusing on local ingredients and Estonian culinary tradition. **Address:** Soola 3, 51013 Tartu (Inside Lydia Hotel). **Price Range: €€** (Mentioned as €€ by the Michelin Guide). **Rating:** Included in the Michelin Guide. Known for its sophisticated and refined approach.

## Joyce

Modern Cuisine, sophisticated and creative. **Address:** Riia 2, 51004 Tartu (Inside V Spa & Conference Hotel). **Price Range: €€** (Mentioned as €€ by the Michelin Guide). **Rating:** Included in the Michelin Guide. Known for its unique atmosphere.

## TOKO

## Restoran Antonius

Solid, dignified food with one ingredient dominating the plate, respectable drinks selection. **Address:** Ülikooli 15, 51003 Tartu (Inside Hotel Antonius). **Price Range: €€€** (Implied "Upper class" / "respectable surroundings"). **Rating:** Included in the Falstaff guide. Known for its discreet and dignified atmosphere.

## Pompei

Simple and concise menu, very tasty. Focus on dishes in harmony with wine. Known for fresh cheeses made in-house. **Address:** Rüütli 7, 51007 Tartu. **Price Range: €€** (Implied by the quality and focus on wine). **Rating:** Included in the Falstaff and White Guide Nordic. A "breath of fresh air" in the culinary landscape.

## Restaurant Fii

Signature cuisine by chef Andrus Saartok. Meticulously crafted dishes, resembling small works of art. Focuses on organic and biodynamic wines. **Address:** Lääneringtee 39, 50501 Tartu linn (Inside Sophia Hotel). **Price Range: €€€** (Implied by "meticulously crafted" and "small works of art"). **Rating:** Included in the Falstaff guide.

## Kampus (Three-in-one)

Three options in one location: Uulits (burgers/Estonian street food), KAUSS (Asian-inspired/healthy bowls), and Peakoka Nurk (slow and fast food). **Address:** Rüütli 7, 51007 Tartu. **Price Range: €** (Good value for money / street food options). **Rating:** Included in the Falstaff and White Guide Nordic. Trendy and unique dining experience.

## Püssirohukelder (Gunpowder Cellar)

Hearty Estonian classics, pub food, Estonian craft beer. **Address:** Lossi 28, 51003 Tartu (Housed in a historic gunpowder store/street icon). **Price Range: €** (Known for "excellent value meals"). **Rating:** Mentioned in Like A Local Guide and TempusTE. Known for being a historical hillside hangout.
