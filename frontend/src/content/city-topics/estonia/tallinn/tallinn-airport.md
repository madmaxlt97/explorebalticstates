# Best ways to get from Tallinn Airport (TLL) to the Tallinn Center.

_Tallinn Airport is conveniently close to the city, making the transfer quick and easy._

## 1. Tram (The Best Public Transport Option)

The tram is the most direct and efficient public transport link connecting the airport to the city center.

Tram Line: Line 4 (Vana-Lõuna to Tondi, passing through the airport and city center).

Stop Location: The stop is located directly in front of the terminal building on the city side. You can walk straight out and into the tram stop.

Route/Destination: The tram goes directly to major stops like Viru Keskus and Hobujaama, which are right in the city center and close to the Old Town.

Travel Time: Approximately _15–17 minutes_.

#### Cost & Tickets:

Single Ticket (Purchased from the driver/conductor): _€2.00_

QR Ticket (Purchased online or via app): _€1.50_

Using a Ühiskaart (Public Transport Card): _€1.50_

#### Note: Public transport is free for registered residents of Tallinn.

## 2. Bus

Buses are also available, often used for connecting to other districts or if the tram is temporarily unavailable.

Bus Line: Bus No. 2 (Mõigu to Reisisadam, passing through the city center).

Stop Location: The bus stop is near the main terminal entrance.

Route/Destination: It goes through the city center to the Passenger Port (Reisisadam).

Travel Time: Approximately _20–25 minutes_ (can vary based on traffic).

Cost & Tickets: The same fare structure as the tram (listed above).

## 3. Taxi / Ride-Sharing

Taxis and ride-sharing apps offer the quickest door-to-door service.

Taxi Stand: Official taxi stands are located directly outside the arrivals hall.

Ride-Sharing Apps: Bolt and Uber are widely used in Tallinn. It is often slightly cheaper to use an app.

Travel Time: Approximately _10–15 minutes_ (without heavy traffic).

#### Estimated Cost: Usually between _€8 – €12_ to the city center/Old Town.

## 4. Rent-A-Car

If you plan to travel outside of Tallinn, you can pick up a rental car inside the terminal.

Travel Time: _10–15 minutes_ drive to the city center.

#### Important Note: Finding parking in the Old Town area can be challenging and expensive.
