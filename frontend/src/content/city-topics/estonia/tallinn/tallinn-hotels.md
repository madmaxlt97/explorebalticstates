# Top Tallinn Hotels: Price/Quality

_Below is a list of hotels in Tallinn that offer an excellent price-to-quality ratio, based on high guest ratings and competitive average prices. (Attention: Prices are approximate average starting prices per night, converted to EUR. Actual prices can change significantly based on the season, booking date, and day of the week. Always check current prices and exact addresses on booking services.)_

## Movenpick Hotel Tallinn

**Area:** Lembitu str. 12. Centre (Kesklinn). **Average Price:** ~€90 - €120. **Rating:** 9.3 / Superb. **Key Highlight:** A highly-rated luxury hotel offering excellent value for money. Located in the city center.

## Palace Hotel Tallinn, a member of Radisson Individuals

**Area:** Vabaduse väljak 3. Centre (Near Freedom Square). **Average Price:** ~€75 - €95. Rating: 9.2 / Wonderful. **Key Highlight:** A high-rated hotel, located a 5-minute walk from the Old Town. Offers comfortable and clean rooms, a gym, a pool, and saunas.

## Citybox Tallinn City Center

**Area:** Laeva str. 1. Sadama (Near Port). **Average Price:** ~€60 - €75. **Rating:** 9.0 / Superb. **Key Highlight:** A stylish and modern hotel with an excellent price-quality ratio. Convenient location near the port and within walking distance of the Old Town.

## Hestia Hotel Europa

**Area:** Paadi str. 5. Centre (Near Port/Old Town). **Average Price:** ~€70 - €90. **Rating:** 8.8 / Fabulous. **Key Highlight:** A highly-rated hotel with modern amenities, popular among guests booking in Tallinn.

## City Hotel Tallinn

**Area:** Paldiski mnt 3-2. Kassissaba (Near City Centre). **Average Price:** ~€55 - €65. **Rating:** 8.6 / Excellent. **Key Highlight:** Great location, very clean rooms and bathrooms, and friendly staff. A good mid-range option.

## Imaginary Hostel

**Area:** Vene str. 33/35-37. Old Town (Vanalinn). **Average Price:** ~€25 - €35 (Budget Choice). **Rating:** 8.0 / Excellent. **Key Highlight:** The best budget option located right in the Old Town. Noted for its very friendly and helpful staff.
