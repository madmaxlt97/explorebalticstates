# Best Traditional Lithuanian Restaurants in Vilnius

## Etno Dvaras

A popular choice for tourists and locals, known for its extensive menu of authentic Lithuanian dishes from different regions, including over ten varieties of cepelinai. It is praised for having the best combination of price, quality, and location. It is conveniently located on Pilies street, right next to Cathedral square. **Price Range:** **€ - €€** (Reasonable/Good prices). **Google Rating:** (Not explicitly available in search results, but highly recommended by many sources).

## Šnekutis

A well-known institution, described as a cheap but cheerful eccentric bar chain serving hearty, traditional "peasant food" and a wide selection of authentic Lithuanian beers ("Village beers"). Has a few locations, one of which is a good spot to eat outdoors in front of a church in good weather. **Description:** Gut-buster meals in a laid-back and casual environment. **Price Range:** **€ - €€** (Inexpensive/Democratic prices). **Location:** Has multiple locations (e.g., in the Old Town). **Google Rating:** (Not explicitly available, but consistently highly rated by Reddit users).

## Lokys (Bear)

An establishment that stands out for its game dishes, offering a more unique traditional Lithuanian experience. It is located near Rotušė square. **Description:** Known for game dishes, implying a more upscale experience. **Price Range:** **€€€** (Higher prices). **Location:** Near Rotušė square (Town Hall Square). **Google Rating:** (Not explicitly available).

## Ertlio Namas

An upscale and intimate restaurant that offers a contemporary fine-dining interpretation of traditional Lithuanian cuisine, with dishes inspired by the food of Lithuanian nobility from the 13th to 19th centuries. They create tasting menus in collaboration with historians. **Description:** Gastronomic journey through the past, combining modern techniques with authentic historical recipes. **Price Range:** **€€€€** (More expensive/Upscale). **Location:** Šv. Jono 7 (conveniently located in Vilnius Old Town, next to Vilnius University). **Google Rating:** (Included in the Michelin Guide, suggesting high quality).

## Senoji Trobelė

A quaint little house that stays true to Lithuania's culinary roots, offering heritage recipes and dishes popular among the nobility, served on clay plates at wooden tables. Known for meat, game, and potato dishes, with many vegetarian options and daily baked traditional bread. **Description:** Authentic eatery with old Lithuanian traditions; very similar to Šnekutis but "a bit fancier". **Price Range:** **€€** (Moderate). **Location:** Naugarduko 36. **Google Rating:** (Not explicitly available).

## Alinė Leičiai

A traditional bar-restaurant that offers traditional peasant food in a more upscale and slightly more expensive environment than some other traditional spots.
