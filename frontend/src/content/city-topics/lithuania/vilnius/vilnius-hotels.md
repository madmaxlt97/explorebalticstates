# Top Vilnius Hotels: Price/Quality

_Below is a list of hotels in Vilnius in three different price levels from low-priced hotels to high-end hotels. Attention: Prices can change significantly based on the season, booking date, and day of the week. Always check current prices and exact addresses on booking services._

## Budget Hotels (High Quality, Low price)

### 1. Calvary Hotel & Restaurant

![Hotel Calvary](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/vilnius/hotel-imgs/calvary-hotel.avif)

**Area:** [Kalvarijų g. 59, Vilnius](https://maps.google.com/?q=Kalvarijų+g.+59+Vilnius) **Average Price:** ~€57 per night. **Rating:** 4.8/5 **Key Highlight:** Located in a beautifully renovated historic building (a former police station), this hotel offers a refined atmosphere, an excellent on-site restaurant, and a modern aesthetic at a very competitive price.

### 2. Domus Maria

![Domus maria Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/vilnius/hotel-imgs/Domus-Maria-Hotel-Vilnius.avif)

**Area:** Aušros Vartų str. 12, Vilnius. **Average Price:** ~€52 per night. **Rating:** 4.6/5. **Key Highlight:** Set in a former 17th-century monastery, it features peaceful courtyards, bright rooms with high ceilings, and includes a complimentary breakfast. It's perfectly located for exploring the historic center.

### 3. City Gate

![City Gate Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/vilnius/hotel-imgs/city-gate-hotel.avif)

**Area:** Bazilijonų str. 3, Vilnius. **Average Price:** ~€45 per night. **Rating:** 4.5/5. **Key Highlight:** A cozy hotel in a 19th-century building. It is known for its friendly service, proximity to the main attractions of the Old Town, and very affordable rates that include breakfast.

## Mid-Range Hotels (Style and Comfort)

### 1. Artagonist Art Hotel

![Artagonist Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/vilnius/hotel-imgs/Artagonist-hotel.avif)

**Area:** Pilies str. 34, Vilnius, **Average Price:** ~€73 per night. **Rating:** 4.7/5. **Key Highlight:** A trendy boutique hotel in an Art Deco building. Each room is uniquely decorated with works by Lithuanian artists. It features a fantastic vintage bar and one of the best locations in the city.

### 2. Shakespeare Boutique Hotel

![Shakespeare Boutique Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/vilnius/hotel-imgs/shakespear-boutique-hotel.avif)

**Area:** Bernardinų str. 8, Vilnius. **Average Price:** ~€70 per night. **Rating:** 4.6/5. **Key Highlight:** A literary-themed hotel where every room is dedicated to a famous author. It offers a very "English" feel with antique furniture, a cozy library atmosphere, and an award-winning restaurant.

### 3. AC Hotel by Marriott Vilnius

![AC By Marriott Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/vilnius/hotel-imgs/AC-by-Marriot-hotel.avif)

**Area:** Vingrių str. 25. **Average Price:** ~€79 per night. **Rating:** 4.6/5. **Key Highlight:** A modern, sleek choice for those who prefer contemporary design. It features a stylish lounge, fitness center, and is within walking distance of the main Gediminas Avenue.

## Luxury Hotels (Top-Tier Elegance)

### 1. Grand Hotel Vilnius, Curio Collection by Hilton

![Grand Hotel Vilnius](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/vilnius/hotel-imgs/Grand-Hotel.avif)
**Area:** Universiteto str. 14. **Average Price:** ~€178 per night. **Rating:** 4.8/5. **Key Highlight:** The most prestigious address in Vilnius. It offers stunning views of the Cathedral, a world-class spa with an indoor pool, and fine dining at the Telegrafas restaurant.

### 2. Hotel PACAI

![PACAI Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/vilnius/hotel-imgs/hotel-pacai.avif)
**Area:** Didžioji str. 7, Vilnius **Average Price:** ~€178 per night. **Rating:** 4.8/5. **Key Highlight:** Housed in a magnificent 17th-century Baroque palace once owned by local nobility. It perfectly blends historical architecture with cutting-edge modern design, featuring a top-tier spa and a high-end restaurant.

### 3. Stikliai Hotel

![Stikliai Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/vilnius/hotel-imgs/hotel-stikliai.avif)
**Area:** Gaono str. 7, Vilnius **Average Price:** ~€157 per night. **Rating:** 4.7/5. **Key Highlight:** A member of Relais & Châteaux, this hotel is the epitome of classic European luxury. It features a beautiful ivy-covered courtyard, a legendary tavern, and rooms decorated with French fabrics and antiques.
