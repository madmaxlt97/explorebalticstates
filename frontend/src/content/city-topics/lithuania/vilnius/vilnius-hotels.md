# Top Vilnius Hotels: Price/Quality

_Below is a list of hotels in Vilnius that offer an excellent price-to-quality ratio, based on high guest ratings and competitive average prices. (Attention: Prices can change significantly based on the season, booking date, and day of the week. Always check current prices and exact addresses on booking services.)._

## Hotel Panorama

**Area:** Seinų g. 4. Old Town. **Average Price:** ~€67. **Rating:** 8.1 / Excellent. **Key Highlight:** Convenient location near the bus/train stations and the entrance to the Old Town. Guests highly praise the cleanliness and breakfast.

## AirInn Vilnius Hotel

**Area:** Rodūnios kl. 8. Vilnius Airport (VNO). **Average Price:** ~€50. **Rating:** 8.6 - 8.9 / Fabulous. **Key Highlight:** Ideal for early or late flights. Excellent service ratings at a mid-range price. Breakfast is often included.

## Amicus Hotel

**Area:** Kaminkelio g. 15. Near Airport (3.1 km from the center). **Average Price:** ~€30. **Rating:** 8.9 / Fabulous. **Key Highlight:** High guest ratings despite the lower price point. Breakfast is often included.

## Hotel Zemaites

**Area:** Žemaitės g. 15. **Average Price:** ~€68. **Rating:** 7.9 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** Offers good quality for its price. Located slightly further from the strict city center.
