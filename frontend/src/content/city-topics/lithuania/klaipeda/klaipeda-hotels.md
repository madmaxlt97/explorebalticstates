# Top Klaipeda Hotels: Price/Quality

_Below is a list of hotels in Klaipeda that offer an excellent price-to-quality ratio, based on high guest ratings and competitive average prices. (Attention: Prices can change significantly based on the season, booking date, and day of the week. Always check current prices and exact addresses on booking services.)._

## Dangė Hotel

**Area:** Liepų str. 38. Centre/Old Town (near Dangė River). **Average Price:** ~€75 - €80. **Rating:** 9.1 / Wonderful. **Key Highlight:** One of the highest-rated hotels in Klaipeda. Great location, modern design, and cleanliness.

## Memel Hotel

**Area:** Bangų str. 4. Centre/Near Old Town. **Average Price:** ~€65 - €70. **Rating:** 9.0 / Wonderful. **Key Highlight:** Stylish and modern hotel with excellent staff reviews. Good price for such a high rating.

## Dunetton - self check-in hotel

**Area:** Jūros str. 23. Centre. **Average Price:** ~€60 - €65. **Rating:** 8.8 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** Self check-in system. Praised for its cleanliness and price/quality ratio. Parking available.

## Old Town Hotel Bohema

**Area:** Galinio Pylimo str. 16. Old Town. **Average Price:** ~€55 - €60. **Rating:** 8.5 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** Budget-friendly option in the historic city centre. A good choice for those who want to be close to main attractions.

## Comfort Hostel Klaipeda

**Area:** Bijūnų str. 2a. Centre. **Average Price:** ~€30 - €40 (for a hostel). **Rating:** 8.4 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** The best option for a very budget-friendly stay. High ratings for a hostel.

## Hotel Navalis

**Area:** H. Manto str. 23. Centre/Port area. **Average Price:** ~€80 - €90. **Rating:** 8.5 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** Marine-themed hotel, popular among guests. Often noted for its good location and service.
