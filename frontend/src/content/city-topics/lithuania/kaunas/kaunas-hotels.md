# Top Kaunas Hotels: Price/Quality

_Below is a list of hotels in Kaunas that offer an excellent price-to-quality ratio, based on high guest ratings and competitive average prices. (Attention: Prices can change significantly based on the season, booking date, and day of the week. Always check current prices and exact addresses on booking services.)._

## Ibis Kaunas Centre

**Area:** Vytauto Av 28 (Centre). **Average Price:** ~€60 (often cited as "Best Value" at ~€40). **Rating:** 8.3 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** Excellent value, often considered the best budget option. Good central location near a bus station and market.

## Moxy Kaunas Center

**Area:** Maironio str. 19 (Centre). **Average Price:** ~€85 (popular at ~€70). **Rating:** 8.8 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** Modern, stylish 3-star boutique hotel in the city center. Highly rated for being new and having excellent customer service.

## Daugirdas Old City Hotel

**Area:** T. Daugirdo str. 4 (Old Town). **Average Price:** ~€78 (cited as "Best Value" at ~€60). **Rating:** 8.3 / Very Good (8.6 Excellent on some sites). **Key Highlight:** Located in the Old Town with panoramic views of the river. Good price for a historic location.

## Olympus Hotel

**Area:** A. Strazdo str. 6 (Centre). **Average Price:** ~€61. **Rating:** 8.8 / Very Good (Excellent on some sites). **Key Highlight:** A cozy, family-run hotel with friendly staff and clean, modern rooms. Very good quality for a low price.

## Amberton Cozy Hotel Kaunas

**Area:** V. Kuzmos str. 8 (Near Old Town). **Average Price:** ~€62 (cited as "Best Value" at ~€55). **Rating:** 8.2 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** Offers a "cozy" atmosphere and is located very close to the Old Town. A reliable and popular choice.

## Hotel Metropolis

**Area:** S. Daukanto str. 21 (Centre). **Average Price:** ~€64 (popular at ~€57). **Rating:** 7.5 / Good (7.9 Very Good on some sites). **Key Highlight:** Fantastic central location. While older, it is often praised for spacious rooms and a great shower. A solid choice for location/price balance.
