# Top Kaunas Hotels: Price/Quality

_Below is a list of hotels in Kaunas in three different price levels from low-priced hotels to high-end hotels. Attention: Prices can change significantly based on the season, booking date, and day of the week. Always check current prices and exact addresses on booking services._

## Budget Hotels (Smart Value)

### 1.Ibis Kaunas Centre

![Ibis Kaunas Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/kaunas/hotel-imgs/ibis-kaunas-hotel.avif)
**Area:** Vytauto Av 28, Kaunas. **Average Price:** ~€50 per night. **Rating:** 4.3/5. **Key Highlight:** A reliable, modern choice within walking distance of the main shopping area and the bus station. It's clean, efficient, and perfect for those who want a standard quality level without surprises.

### 2. Hotel Metropolis

![Metropolis Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/kaunas/hotel-imgs/metropolis-hotel.avif)
**Area:** S. Daukanto str. 21, Kaunas. **Average Price:** ~€45 per night. **Rating:** 4.1/5. **Key Highlight:** A legendary historic hotel located right on the famous pedestrian boulevard. While the rooms are simpler, the history and the location are unbeatable for the price.

### 3. Magnus Hotel

![Magnus Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/kaunas/hotel-imgs/magnus-hotel.avif)
**Area:** Vytauto Av 25, Kaunas. **Average Price:** ~€50 per night. **Rating:** 4.2/5. **Key Highlight:** Offers a great panoramic view of the city from its upper floors. It’s a straightforward, comfortable hotel with easy access to public transport and the Zalgiris Arena.

## Mid-Range Hotels (Style & Convenience)

### 1. Moxy Kaunas Center

![Moxy Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/kaunas/hotel-imgs/moxy-hotel.avif)
**Area:** Maironio str. 19, Kaunas. **Average Price:** ~€70. **Rating:** 4.4/5. **Key Highlight:** A Marriott lifestyle brand hotel with a very playful, social vibe. It features a 24/7 bar that doubles as a check-in desk, modern compact rooms, and a high-energy atmosphere perfect for younger travelers.

### 2. HOF Hotel

![HOF Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/kaunas/hotel-imgs/HOF hotel.avif)
**Area:** Maironio str. 21A, Kaunas. **Average Price:** ~€60 per night. **Rating:** 4.4/5. **Key Highlight:** hidden gem that is tucked away from the main street noise. It offers spacious, modern rooms and a more personalized, boutique feel than the larger chain hotels.

### 3. Daugirdas Old City Hotel

![Daugirdas Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/kaunas/hotel-imgs/daugirdas.avif)
**Area:** T. Daugirdo str. 4, Kaunas. **Average Price:** ~€70 per night.**Rating:** 4.4/5. **Key Highlight:** Set in a restored 16th-century building, this hotel blends authentic brickwork with modern amenities. It has a fantastic terrace and is just steps away from the Town Hall Square.

## Luxury / High-End (Premier Experience)

### 1. Monte Pacis

![Monte Pacis Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/kaunas/hotel-imgs/monte-pacis.avif)

**Area:** T. Masiulio str. 31, Kaunas. **Average Price:** ~€70 - €120+ per night.**Rating:** 4.5/5. **Key Highlight:** A unique hospitality experience located within a functioning Baroque monastery complex. It is incredibly peaceful, with a fine-dining restaurant that is considered one of the best in Lithuania.

### 2. Radisson Hotel Kaunas

![Radisson Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/kaunas/hotel-imgs/radisson.avif)
**Area:** K. Donelaičio str. 27, Kaunas, **Average Price:** ~€102 per night.**Rating:** 4.5/5. **Key Highlight:** Recently renovated and the largest high-end hotel in the city. It offers a Scandinavian design, an excellent spa center, a casino, and a high-quality breakfast buffet.

### 3. Viešbutis KAUNAS Hotel

![Viešbutis Kaunas Hotel](/assets/images/topics-images/lithuania/kaunas/hotel-imgs/viesbutis-Kaunas.avif)
**Area:** Laisvės Av 79, Kaunas, **Average Price:** ~€113 per night.**Rating:** 4.5/5. **Key Highlight:** An elegant, classic hotel located directly on the main pedestrian street. It features a fitness center, an indoor pool, and rooms with balconies overlooking the bustling boulevard.
