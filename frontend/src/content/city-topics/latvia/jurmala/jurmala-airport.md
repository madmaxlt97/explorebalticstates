# Best ways to reach Jurmalas center from Rigas (RIX) airport.

_Jūrmala is a popular resort town located close to Riga, making the transfer from the airport quick and easy. The center of Jūrmala is often considered the Majori area, known for its main pedestrian street._

## 1. Direct Local Train (Most Convenient)

This is the easiest route, as you can take the train directly from the station near the airport to the heart of Jūrmala.

### Step 1: Airport to Train Station:

From the RIX Arrivals Hall, take a short shuttle bus (or walk about _10-15_ minutes) to the "Priedaine" train station.

#### Note: A new railway station at the airport is planned, but for now, Priedaine is the closest option.

### Step 2: Train to Jūrmala:

Board the train towards Sloka or Tukums II.

Get off at the "Majori" station (the central stop in Jūrmala).

Total Duration: Approximately _40–50 minutes_ (including transfer time).

#### Cost: Train fare is typically around _€1.40 – €2.00_.

## 2. Taxi/Ride-Sharing

The quickest door-to-door option, ideal if traveling with luggage.

Departure: Taxi rank outside the Arrivals Hall or via Bolt/Uber app.

Arrival: Your desired location in Jūrmala (e.g., Majori or Dzintari).

Duration: Approximately _20–30 minutes_.

#### Estimated Cost: _€25 – €40_, depending on traffic and time of day.
