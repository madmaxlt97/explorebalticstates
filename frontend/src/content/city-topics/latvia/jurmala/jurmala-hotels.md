# Top Jūrmala Hotels: Price/Quality

_Below is a list of hotels in Jūrmala that offer an excellent price-to-quality ratio, based on high guest ratings and competitive average prices. (Attention: Prices are approximate average starting prices per night, converted to EUR. Actual prices can change significantly based on the season, booking date, and day of the week. Always check current prices and exact addresses on booking services.)_

## Tamara Suites & Apartments

**Area:** Jomas str. 83. Dzintari. **Average Price:** ~€63. **Rating:** 9.2 / Exceptional. **Key Highlight:** Highly-rated, modern suites and apartments offering excellent value, cleanliness, and comfort in a quieter Jūrmala area.

## Marine Hotel Jurmala

**Area:** Poruka prospekts 12. Melluži. **Average Price:** ~€70. **Rating:** 9.2 / Exceptional. **Key Highlight:** Offers high-quality accommodation with an exceptional rating, providing a comfortable stay close to the seaside.

## Boutique Hotel Mama

**Area:** Tirgoņu str. 22. Majori. **Average Price:** ~€53 - €63. **Rating:** 8.3 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** A stylish boutique option that delivers good value for its location near the famous Jomas Street and the beach.

## Lielupe Hotel SPA & Conferences by Semarah

**Area:** Bulduru prospekts 64-68. Bulduri. **Average Price:** ~€67 - €72. **Rating:** 9.1–9.4 / Wonderful. **Key Highlight:** A large, modern hotel featuring a full-service SPA and conference facilities. Excellent choice for wellness and family trips.

## Villa Joma

**Area:** Jomas str. 90. Majori. **Average Price:** ~€73 - €83. **Rating:** 9.4 / Wonderful. **Key Highlight:** Exceptional guest rating and service in a charming building right on the central, pedestrian Jomas Street.

## Hotel Jurmala Spa

**Area:** Jomas str. 47/49. Majori. **Average Price:** ~€82 - €104. **Rating:** 8.7 / Excellent. **Key Highlight:** One of the most popular spa hotels, centrally located, offering extensive wellness and health services.

## Baltic Beach Hotel & SPA

**Area:** Jūras iela 23/25. Majori. **Average Price:** ~€152 - €170. **Rating:** 8.7–8.8 / Excellent (Luxury). **Key Highlight:** The premier 5-star beachfront luxury hotel, featuring a large marine SPA complex right on the coast.
