# Top Riga Hotels: Price/Quality

_Below is a list of hotels in Riga that offer an excellent price-to-quality ratio, based on high guest ratings and competitive average prices. (Attention: Prices can change significantly based on the season, booking date, and day of the week. Always check current prices and exact addresses on booking services.)_

## Aston Hotel Riga

**Area:** Brīvības str. 48/50. City Center. **Average Price:** ~€65 - €75. **Rating:** 9.1 / Wonderful. **Key Highlight:** Highly-rated modern hotel with a fantastic breakfast, excellent cleanliness, and a safe, central location.

## Rixwell Old Riga Palace Hotel

**Area:** Minsterejas str. 8-10. Old Town (Vecrīga). **Average Price:** ~€40 - €50. **Rating:** 8.0 / Very Good. **Key Highlight:** Excellent value budget option right in the Old Town. One of the best-value deals found in search results.

## Hampton by Hilton Riga Airport

**Area:** Priezkalni A. Airport. **Average Price:** ~€65 - €75. **Rating:** 9.1 / Wonderful. **Key Highlight:** Excellent cleanliness, comfortable beds, and a great breakfast at an affordable price point for the brand. Perfect for early flights.

## Wellton Riverside SPA Hotel

**Area:** 11. novembra krastmala 33. Old Town (Vecrīga). **Average Price:** ~€80 - €90. **Rating:** 8.8 / Excellent. **Key Highlight:** A 5-star property with SPA facilities for a moderate price. Praised for good service and large rooms in a great location.

## Konventa Sēta Hotel Keystone Collection

**Area:** Kalēju str. 9/11. Old Town (Vecrīga). **Average Price:** ~€55 - €65. **Rating:** 9.0 / Wonderful. **Key Highlight:** High-rated hotel in the Old Town area, offering great convenience and quality for the price.

## Mosaic Hotel

**Area:** Elizabetes str. 31A. Centre (Near Art Nouveau District/Alberta Street). **Average Price:** ~€35 - €45 (Budget Choice). **Rating:** (High for its price range). **Key Highlight:** One of the best cheap options, great for budget travelers who want to be near the city center and Art Nouveau sights.
